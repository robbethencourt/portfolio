flakas

Logo, apron and business card for Flaka's Baked Goods. My wife is also a baker, and this is her baking business.

logo / print design
created 2012

Logo

A hand drawn logo that I then took into Illustrator.

Apron & Business Card

This was a gift. I wanted her to have an apron with the company logo on it. Also, baking is really messy, so this keeps her clean, even if the rest of the kitchen is a wreck.

Labels & My Contact Info

The business cards are meant to attach to each baked good and can be written on to describe the "Delicious Treat" without having to make labels. It also allows her to put her contact information on each card so that if someone gives them as gifts to others, they know who to contact if they want some more!
